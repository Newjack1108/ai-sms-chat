# 📖 Simple Guide: Timesheet & Holidays

---

## ⏰ TIMESHEET - Clock In/Out

### 🟢 Clock In (Start Work)

Before your first departure, complete the **Daily Vehicle Check** on the timesheet page.

**Step 1:** Open Timesheet page  
**Step 2:** Fill in Daily Vehicle Check (and trailer checks if towing)  
**Step 3:** Save the check  
**Step 4:** Pick your job/site from the list  
**Step 5:** Click **"Clock In"**  
**Step 6:** Allow location access (or click Continue)

✅ **Done!** You'll see green "Clocked In" status

---

### 🔴 Clock Out (Finish Work)

**Step 1:** Click **"Clock Out"** button  
**Step 2:** Done! Your time is saved

⚠️ **Remember:** Only one clock in/out per day

---

### 🚐 Daily Vehicle And Trailer Check

Complete this once per day before leaving to site.

**What to do:**
- Enter vehicle registration
- Complete all vehicle safety checks
- If towing, tick **Trailer attached** and complete trailer checks
- Add notes if needed and click **Save Daily Check**

If any critical item is marked **Fail**, your check is still saved but a warning is logged for manager follow-up.

---

### 📅 View Your Week

**Scroll down** to see your weekly timesheet

**Use arrows** ← → to see other weeks

**Each day shows:**
- ⏰ When you clocked in/out
- 📊 Your hours (regular, overtime, weekend, overnight)
- 📝 What you did (activities)
- 🏷️ Day type (holiday, sick day, etc.)

---

### ➕ Add Activity (What You Did)

**Step 1:** Find the day (current week only)  
**Step 2:** Click **"+ Add Activity"**  
**Step 3:** Type what you did (e.g., "Installing panels")  
**Step 4:** Click **"Save"**

💡 You can add many activities per day  
❌ Click × to delete an activity

---

### 🏠 Overnight Away

**If you stayed away from home:**

**Step 1:** Find the day  
**Step 2:** Check **"Overnight away from home"** box

✅ This adds extra pay to your timesheet

---

### 🏷️ Day Type

**For days you didn't work:**

**Step 1:** Find the day  
**Step 2:** Pick from dropdown:
- Normal Day
- Paid Holiday
- Unpaid Holiday  
- Paid Sick Day
- Unpaid Sick Day

---

### 🔧 Fix Mistakes

#### ❌ Forgot to Clock In/Out?

**Step 1:** Find the day  
**Step 2:** Click **"Add Missing Times"**  
**Step 3:** Pick job/site  
**Step 4:** Enter correct times  
**Step 5:** Say why you forgot  
**Step 6:** Click **"Submit Request"**

⏰ Manager will review it

---

#### ✏️ Wrong Times?

**Step 1:** Find the day  
**Step 2:** Click **"Edit Times"**  
**Step 3:** Enter correct times  
**Step 4:** Say why you're changing it  
**Step 5:** Click **"Submit Request"**

⏰ Manager will approve it

⚠️ **Note:** Can only edit times up to 10 days old

---

### ⚠️ Important Rules

- ❌ Only clock in/out **once per day**
- ❌ Can only edit times **up to 10 days old**
- ✅ Manager must approve all changes
- ✅ System calculates pay rates automatically
- 📍 Your location is saved (for security)

---

## 🏖️ HOLIDAY REQUESTS

### 👀 Check Your Days

**Open Holiday Requests page**

**You'll see:**
- 📊 **Total Days** - How many you have this year
- ✅ **Days Used** - How many you've taken
- ⏳ **Days Left** - How many remaining

---

### 📝 Request Holiday

**Step 1:** Fill in the form:
- 📅 **Start Date** - First day off
- 📅 **End Date** - Last day off
- 📋 **Day Type**:
  - Full Day (whole day off)
  - Half Day (half day - start and end must be same date)

**Step 2:** System shows:
- How many days you're asking for
- How many you'll have left

**Step 3:** Click **"Submit Request"**

✅ Manager will review it

---

### 📋 View Your Requests

**Scroll down** to see your requests

**Status colors:**
- 🟡 **PENDING** - Waiting for approval
- 🟢 **APPROVED** - Yes! You got it
- 🔴 **REJECTED** - Not approved (see reason)
- ⚪ **CANCELLED** - You cancelled it

---

### ❌ Cancel Request

**Step 1:** Find your request  
**Step 2:** Click **"Cancel"** button  
**Step 3:** Confirm

✅ Days go back to your balance

---

### ⚠️ Important Rules

- ❌ Only request **weekdays** (Mon-Fri)
- ❌ Half day = start and end date **must be same**
- ❌ Need enough days left in your balance
- ✅ Manager must approve all requests
- ✅ Approved holidays show in timesheet
- ✅ System skips weekends automatically

---

### 💡 Tips

- 📅 **Plan early** - Popular dates fill up fast
- 💰 **Check balance** - Make sure you have enough days
- ⏰ **Half days** - Save days for longer breaks
- 👀 **Read notes** - If rejected, see why
